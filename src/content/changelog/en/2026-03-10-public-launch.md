Thanks to the 38 stables that tested Hovera over the past six months. Their feedback became the product we present today.

## What you get in 1.0

- 7 product modules (Calendar, Passes, Invoicing+e-Invoicing, Journal, Mobile, Booking, AI in beta)
- 4 pricing plans (Free, Solo, Stable, Pro)
- Native iOS + Android apps
- Full KSeF Type 2 integration
- Peppol BIS Billing 3.0 for the EU
- Public API + webhooks (Pro)
