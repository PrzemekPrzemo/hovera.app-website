## What's new

- Full offline-write — every action available without network
- Conflict detection (e.g. someone changed the same record offline and online)
- 'X changes pending sync' indicator
- Apple Watch support (alert about an upcoming ride)

## Small fixes

- Faster horse list with 100+ records
- Fixed iOS 18 beta calendar bug
