## Horstable

**Site:** [horstable.com](https://horstable.com)
**Scale:** 1 400+ riders, 400+ instructors (May 2026 per their site)
**Price:** Starter free / Mini €18 / Midi €40 / Maxi €70

### Strengths

- **Very good mobile app** — native iOS + Android, good UX, real App Store reviews
- **1:1 onboarding** — Horstable team personally helps with setup. One of the best onboardings in the industry.
- **Clean UX for school** — UI designed for riding school, not generic "horse management"
- **Real traction** — 1400+ riders use it, system is proven

### Weaknesses

- **School-only** — no boarding, no breeding, no real sport. A 30-horse boarding stable has no offer.
- **Client limits per plan** — Mini 30 clients, Midi 90, only Maxi unlimited. The bigger you grow, the more you pay even if you don't need Maxi features.
- **No owner portal** (key for boarding)
- **No public API** — bookkeeping integrations are manual

### When to pick

- Small to mid-sized riding school (up to 100 clients)
- Where 1:1 rollout is a priority (less technical owners)
- Kids/family client base (UX optimized for that segment)

### When NOT to pick

- Significant boarding (15+ boarded horses)
- Breeding stable
- Multi-disciplinary operation (sport + school + livery)

## Nasza Stajnia

**Site:** [nasza-stajnia.pl](https://nasza-stajnia.pl)
**Price:** €15 / €30 / mo

### Strengths

- **Cheapest** PL system
- **SMS bundle** — 200/400 messages monthly included
- **Strong staff/payroll module**
- **Polish support**

### Weaknesses

- **No native mobile app** — web-only
- **Smaller team** — slower update cadence
- **No boarding module**
- **UX less polished** than Horstable or Hovera

### When to pick

- Very tight budget
- SMS-heavy client communication
- Complex staff payroll

### When NOT to pick

- Field staff want phone-based work (no app)
- International ambitions (PL-only)

## Equicty (Belgium)

**Site:** [equicty.com](https://www.equicty.com)
**Price:** ~€50-150/mo

### Strengths

- **Widest feature set** in Europe — school, livery, breeding, sport, racing
- **Hoofy AI assistant** — first AI chatbot in the industry (since 2024)
- **Smart Stable Board** — dedicated 32" touchscreen for the stable (optional hardware)
- **Mature** — 8+ years on the market
- **Multi-discipline** — works for many stable types

### Weaknesses

- **No Polish language**
- **Support only EN, FR, NL**
- **Significantly higher price** (€50-150 = €600-1800/year)
- **Hardware investment** for full value (Smart Stable Board ~€3-5k)
- **Complex onboarding** (better for technical users)

### When to pick

- Stable expanding to BE/NL/DE
- Premium operation with €5k+/year tooling budget
- Multi-discipline center wanting all-in-one
- Values AI features

### When NOT to pick

- Stable with B2B invoicing in Poland (no KSeF)
- Smaller scale (€10-30/mo budget)
- Daily English not comfortable

## BarnManager and StableSecretary (USA)

Brief — for most European stables they aren't practical:

**BarnManager** — mature US-centric system. Strong reports, good mobile, but:
- No EU compliance (GDPR issue with US hosting)
- No EU e-invoicing
- EN support, US time zone
- ~$30-80/mo

**StableSecretary** — very good for horse health + billing, but:
- US-centric
- No EU compliance
- ~$30/mo

**For:** European clients hosting horses in the USA (rare) or with English-only teams.
